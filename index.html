Welcome!

Whether you're an avid reader or you've never picked up a book in your life, you've come to the right place. Here, you'll be able to explore options for recreational reading. I won't be reviewing informational books, and I will only briefly cover non-fiction. However, there will be a lot of discussion on fictional genres. Also, the primary target audience for these books will be young adults, but anyone can read and enjoy them. Have fun!

Do you have trouble picking up a book? It is definitely difficult to become interested in reading after years, or even months. This page will help you get started and explore your interests. Click the icon to get started!
